We have decided to deprecate those social login options because of several reasons:
- Privacy: every time you sign in using one of these methods, the respective company knows and we have no information on what they do with this data.
- Not fully open-standard compatible: these platforms enjoy their monopolies and don't play well with open standards like OAuth2/OpenID: in fact, implementations on mobile of these social login require their SDK that we refuse to add because of privacy concern.
- Uniform experiences for all users: to have these social login in our iOS app, we need to support "Sign in with Apple" that isn't broadly available for Android users. Again, another big tech enjoying its monopoly.
If you happen to use one of these social login options, please create a password for your account on the Setting page
You can still sign in using these social login until 2020-05-31. After this date, they will be removed. {% endcall %} {% call text() %} 5) WebAuthn (Beta)
